X Maths Complete

This course covers all the topics of High School Mathematics. These topics form the foundation of Senior High School Mathematics and these combined together makes the base of engineering mathematics. Engineering Mathematics will be extrememly helpful in your career. It has applications in many fields including Data Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Software Development, Financial Modelling and much more.

Based on the diverse applications of topics covered in this course, the target audience for this course will also include working professionals in the above-mentioned fields and many more. So, please let us know if you find our course helpful in your work or projects.

About the Course

In these course, we will cover seven units, namely, Number Systems, Algebra, Coordinate Geometry, Trigonometry, Mensuration, Statistics and Probability. The total marks covered by these topics are 80.

1) The unit Number Systems contains the chapter Real Numbers and covers 6 marks.

2) The unit Algebra contains the chapters Polynomials, Pair of Linear Equations in Two Variables, Quadratic Equations, Arithmetic and Progressions, and covers 20 marks.

3) The unit Coordinate Geometry contains the chapter Lines in Two Dimensions and covers 6 marks.

4) The unit Geometry contains the chapters Triangles, Circles and Constructions, and covers 15 marks.

5) The unit Trigonometry contains the chapters Introduction to Trigonometry, Trigonometric Identities and Heights & Distances, and covers 12 marks.

6) The unit Mensuration contains the chapters Areas Related to Circles and Surface Areas & Volumes, and covers 10 marks.

7) The unit Statistics & Proabability contains the chapters Statistics and Probability, and covers 11 marks.

Course Overview
8 Units
This course has 8 units divided into 16 chapters. Chapters are further divided into lessons to cover the topics in a detailed and clear manner.
4 Terms Exams and 16 Quizzes
These Exams and Quizzes are designed to help you practice and apply the skills you learn.
Previous Year problems
Previous 10 years papers are solved to understand the pattern and trend of questions asked in the exam.
